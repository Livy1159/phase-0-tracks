- Wait until the elevator door opens in front of you.
- Walk forward until you are in the middle of the elevator.
- Sort through the numbers on the buttons.
- If that number is the floor you want:
	- Push the button.
	- Quit sorting.
	- Wait for the elevator to reach your floor.
	- Get off the elevator.
- Otherwise:
	- Keep sorting.

Picking out the right size of jeans.

-Pick up the first pair of jeans and look at the size.
-If this is your size:
	- Pick up the jeans.
	- Quit sorting.
	- Put the jeans on.
- Otherwise:
	- keep sorting.
